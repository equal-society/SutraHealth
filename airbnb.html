Appreciate the herbal garden, join Yoga classes under the sky led by the host — a qualified MBBS doctor and Yoga instructor — stroll or cycle in Aravalli forests, visit temples and parks nearby. Spread out in the study, read books in the reading nook, or relax in the living room.

Just 1.5 km from Asian Institute of Medical Sciences (AIMS), conveniently close to two metro stations with easy access to South Delhi and beyond. Wide, clean roads and lift access make this the perfect base for short or long stays.
