Importance of Lifestyle Medicine Research

Lifestyle medicine studies how daily habits such as nutrition, physical activity, sleep, stress management and behaviour changes affect overall health. Research helps create preventive strategies for managing lifestyle related health conditions and improving quality of life.

Research Areas

Lifestyle Medicine for IBS

Research focuses on digestive health improvement through personalized nutrition, stress management, gut health support and lifestyle changes.

The goal is reducing IBS symptoms and improving daily wellness.

Lifestyle Medicine for Hypertension

Studies explore how diet, exercise, weight management and stress control can support healthy blood pressure management.

Lifestyle strategies work together with medical care for better health outcomes.

Lifestyle Medicine for Diabetes

Research explores nutrition planning, physical activity, sleep improvement and lifestyle behaviour changes for diabetes management.

The focus is improving metabolic health through sustainable habits.

Menstrual Health Research

Research focuses on women's wellness, hormonal health, nutrition and lifestyle factors affecting menstrual wellbeing.

Preventive healthcare approaches support better awareness and healthy lifestyle practices.

Doctor's Message

"Healthcare should focus not only on treating disease but also on preventing illness. Lifestyle medicine empowers people to make informed decisions and create healthier lives."
