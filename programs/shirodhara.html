Ayurvedic Treatments

Abhyanga Massage

Abhyanga massage, also called Snehana, means "to nurture with love." Warm herbal oils are used to massage the body with special focus on the forehead, temples, ears, palms and soles where important Marma points are present.

This therapy helps improve circulation, relieve stress, rejuvenate the body and calm the nervous system naturally.

Shirodhara Therapy

Shirodhara comes from the Sanskrit words "Shiro" meaning head and "Dhara" meaning flow.

In this therapy, warm herbal oil, milk, buttermilk or water is gently poured onto the forehead in a rhythmic stream while the body relaxes deeply.

The STHAPNI Marma on the forehead gets stimulated, helping calm the mind, balance emotions and relax the hypothalamus.
