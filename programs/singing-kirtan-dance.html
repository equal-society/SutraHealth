Singing, Kirtan and Dance

Music, devotion and movement together create joy, positivity and emotional healing. Singing, Kirtan and Dance uplift the mind, energize the body and connect people through happiness and harmony.

Healing Through Music & Movement

Singing has many health benefits, including focusing attention away from worries, exercise of lungs and stimulates Thyroid gland, thereby elevating mood and positivity.

Group Singing brings social support and belonging to all, and creates positive vibrations. Singing chants in praise of God, called Kirtan, is the highest form of collective singing which uplifts the soul besides the mind.

Dancing is an excellent way to express one's emotions and tone up the body. Each of these health enhancing acts can be done at any age.
