About Us

About EQUAL Society

Effort For Quality of Life (EQUAL) Society is a not-for-profit organization committed to the attainment of highest quality of life by all.

Registered under the Societies Registration Act, 1860 in Haryana vide no. 363, dated 28.5.1997, and recognized under 12A and 80G of the Income Tax Act.

EQUAL Society believes in holistic development of individuals and communities through health, education, empowerment, awareness and sustainable living.

Aims & Objectives

Our work spans the full continuum — from prevention to rehabilitation, from individual to community.

Health Advancement

Advancement of health by rendering preventive, promotive, curative and rehabilitative services to all.

Family Welfare

Promotion of maternal and child health, immunization and community awareness programs.

Quality of Life

Improving opportunities for education, nutrition, employment and healthy living across communities.

Human Values

Promoting dignity, equality, awareness and humanitarian service as the foundation of our work.
