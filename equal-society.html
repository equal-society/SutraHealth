About EQUAL Society

Effort For Quality of Life (EQUAL) Society is a not-for-profit organization committed to the attainment of highest quality of Life by All.

It is registered under Societies Registration Act, 1860 in Haryana vide no. 363, dated 28.5.1997, under 12A and 80G of Income Tax Act.

EQUAL Society believes in holistic development of individuals and communities through health, education, empowerment, awareness and sustainable living.
